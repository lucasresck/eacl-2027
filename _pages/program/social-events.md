<h1>Welcome Reception</h1>


<p>EACL 2026 Welcome Reception<br>
<br>
<strong>March 24, 2026</strong> | <strong>18:00–20:00</strong> (6:00 PM–8:00 PM)<br>
<strong>Venue:</strong> Palais des Congrès Rabat Bouregreg, Rabat, Morocco
</p>

<p>
Kick off EACL 2026 with an evening of conversation, connection, and celebration in beautiful Rabat.
Join fellow researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders as we begin the conference together.
</p>

<h2>Event schedule</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>18:00 – Doors open</strong><br>
  Arrival, registration check-in, welcome drinks, and light canapés served.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Reception details</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Light canapés will be served throughout the evening.</li>
  <li>Each registered attendee will receive <strong>one complimentary drink ticket</strong>.</li>
  <li>Additional beverages will be available.</li>
  <li>Guest and on-site registration will be available at the venue.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Transportation</h3>
<p>
Shuttle buses will run from a central location in Rabat to the conference center before and after the reception.

Please check the <strong>updated transportation schedule on Whova or the official conference website</strong> for the latest pickup times and details.
</p>

<h3>Beat the rush – register early</h3>
<p>
Register early to avoid delays and make the most of the evening. Arrive early and enjoy the beautiful park located just
across the street from the venue before the reception begins.
</p>

<h2>Important information</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Attendance is included for all Main Conference registrants.</li>
  <li>If you are not registered for the Main Conference but would like to attend, you may add this event to your registration at the Registration Solution Desk located on the lobby level.</li>
  
</ul>
