<h1>EACL 2027 Call for Main Conference Papers</h1>

The European Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics (EACL) invites submissions of original, high-quality research on all aspects of natural language processing and computational linguistics.

Our Program Committee is currently finalizing the comprehensive Call for Papers and detailed timetable for EACL 2027. While we prepare the full list of topics and submission policies, we are sharing the primary submission deadline below so you can organize your research timeline. 

We encourage prospective authors to familiarize themselves with the [ARR author guidelines](https://aclrollingreview.org/authors) as they prepare their manuscripts.

### Important Dates

- **ARR submission deadline (long & short papers):** August 3, 2026 (AoE)

*Please check back soon for the complete Call for Papers.*
